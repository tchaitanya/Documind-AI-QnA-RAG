## 2. Time Off and Vacation Policy

### Vacation Days

Full-time employees receive the following vacation days per year based on tenure:

- **0-2 years**: 15 days per year
- **3-5 years**: 20 days per year
- **6+ years**: 25 days per year

### Requesting Time Off

1. Submit a time-off request at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Requests must be approved by your direct manager
3. Use the HR portal to submit requests
4. Emergency time off should be communicated immediately

### Sick Leave

- Employees receive 10 sick days per year
- Unused sick days do not roll over
- Medical documentation may be required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days

### Holidays

The company observes the following holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Day

## 3. Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility

Remote work is available to employees who:
- Have been with the company for at least 6 months
- Maintain satisfactory performance ratings
- Have a suitable home office setup
- Receive manager approval

### Requirements

1. **Equipment**: Company provides laptop and necessary equipment
2. **Connectivity**: Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 25 Mbps)
3. **Availability**: Must be available during core hours (9 AM - 3 PM)
4. **Communication**: Respond to messages within 2 hours during business hours

### Hybrid Schedule

- Office presence required 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday)
- Team meetings typically held on Tuesdays
- Fully remote work requires VP approval

## 4. Expense Reimbursement

### Eligible Expenses

The following business-related expenses are reimbursable:

- **Travel**: Airfare, lodging, ground transportation
- **Meals**: Business meals (up to $75 per day)
- **Office Supplies**: Work-related supplies and equipment
- **Professional Development**: Approved training and conferences

### Submission Process

1. Submit expenses within 30 days of incurrence
2. Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25
3. Use the expense management system
4. Manager approval required for all submissions

### Reimbursement Timeline

- Approved expenses processed within 10 business days
- Direct deposit to employee bank account
- Questions? Contact finance@company.com

## 5. Code of Conduct

### Core Values

Our organization is built on:
- **Integrity**: Act honestly and ethically
- **Respect**: Treat everyone with dignity
- **Excellence**: Strive for quality in all work
- **Collaboration**: Work together effectively
- **Innovation**: Embrace new ideas and approaches

### Professional Behavior

All employees must:
- Maintain professional conduct at all times
- Respect diversity and inclusion
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Protect confidential information
- Report violations to HR immediately

### Consequences

Violations of the code of conduct may result in:
- Verbal or written warning
- Mandatory training
- Suspension
- Termination
